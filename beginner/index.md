# Beginner

This section is for people who have just started playing; good for those playing at around level 5 or below.

Much of the information here isn't super necessary to know right off the bat: but, as you make your way through, and play certain charts, you might have a few questions on certain more obscure parts of the game. This area serves to answer those questions.

In summary, this section will help you learn the following:

- Information on certain options, unlocks and note types
- Some other generally good advice that is good to know
